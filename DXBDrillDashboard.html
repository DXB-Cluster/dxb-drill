DCEO Performance Grading Framework
GREEN — Proficient
  • Responds within SLA ≤ 5 min
  • Identifies fault without prompting
  • Follows LOTO / EPO precisely
  • Clear structured communication
  • Escalates correct dept within 2 min
  • Correct MOP/SOP cited and followed
  • Zero deviations or safety violations
≥ 85%
YELLOW — Developing
  • Responds with minor hesitation
  • Identifies fault with minor prompting
  • Follows key steps, misses 1–2 items
  • Communication adequate but unstructured
  • Escalation delayed 2–5 min
  • MOP/SOP referenced, some steps missed
  • 1–2 minor deviations, no safety risk
65–84%
RED — Needs Improvement
  • Response time exceeds SLA
  • Fails to identify fault independently
  • Key safety or LOTO steps skipped
  • Miscommunication or no escalation
  • SOP not followed or not referenced
  • Multiple safety violations or deviations
  • Re-drill required
< 65%
